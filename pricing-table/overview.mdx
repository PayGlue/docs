## What is the PayGlue pricing table?

The PayGlue pricing table lets you display your payment provider's products directly on your Ghost site, with buy buttons that link to your provider's checkout. No Stripe required for the checkout flow.

## Features

- 1 to 5 pricing columns
- Works with Polar and other supported providers
- Embeddable via a simple code snippet in Ghost
- Fully customizable labels, descriptions, and feature lists
