---
title: "Product Mapping"
description: "Connect a payment provider product to a Ghost membership tier."
---

A **product mapping** tells PayGlue: when a customer buys product X from provider Y, give them Ghost membership tier Z and send welcome email type W.

## Where mappings are created

Mappings are not created on a dedicated page. Instead, you configure them inline when you set up a **Buy Button**, **Paywall**, or **Pricing Table**. When you select a product and a Ghost tier in one of those editors and click **Save**, the mapping is recorded automatically.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open an editor">
    Go to **Installation \> Buttons**, **Paywall**, or **Pricing Table** in your PayGlue dashboard and open or create an item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a provider and product">
    Choose your payment provider and select the product. Choose **Link a product** as the link type. Select your payment provider and the product you want to map.

    <Frame>
      ![Payglue Product Mapping](/images/payglue-product-mapping.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Ghost settings">
    Choose what happens after the customer buys a product

    <Frame>
      ![Payglue Ghost Cms Mapping](/images/payglue-ghost-cms-mapping.png)
    </Frame>

    1. One-Time or Subscription
    2. Subscribe to the newsletter. (_Defaul: Yes_)
    3. Welcome email for new member (_Magic link is recommended.)_
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure email">
    Select which welcome email Ghost should send. Magic link is recommended.

    <Frame>
      ![Payglue Welcome Email Actions](/images/payglue-welcome-email-actions.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and copy the webhook URL">
    Save the mapping. Copy the webhook URL and add it to your provider's webhook settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The mapping is created automatically and appears in **Analytics \> Product Mapping**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing all mappings

Go to **Analytics \> Product Mapping** in your dashboard to see all configured mappings — which product maps to which Ghost tier, the trigger event type, and where each mapping was created (button, paywall, or pricing table).

<Frame>
  ![Payglue Analytics](/images/payglue-analytics.png)
</Frame>

You cannot edit or delete mappings directly from this view. To change a mapping, open the Buy Button, Paywall, or Pricing Table item it belongs to and update the settings there.
