---
title: "Product Mapping"
description: "Connect a payment provider product to a Ghost membership tier."
---

A **product mapping** tells PayGlue: when a customer buys product X from provider Y, give them Ghost membership tier Z and send welcome email type W.

## Where mappings are created

Mappings are configured inline when you set up a **Buy Button**, **Paywall**, or **Pricing Table**. When you select a product and a Ghost tier in one of those editors and click **Save**, the mapping is recorded automatically. There is no separate "create mapping" flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open an editor">
    Go to **Installation > Buttons**, **Paywall**, or **Pricing Table** in your PayGlue dashboard and open or create an item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link a product">
    Choose **Link a product** as the link type. Select your payment provider and the product you want to map.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Ghost settings">
    Select the Ghost newsletter (tier) the member should receive and the welcome email type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The mapping is created automatically and appears in **Analytics > Product Mapping**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing all mappings

Go to **Analytics > Product Mapping** in your dashboard to see all configured mappings — which product maps to which Ghost tier, the trigger event type, and where each mapping was created (button, paywall, or pricing table).

You cannot edit or delete mappings directly from this view. To change a mapping, open the Buy Button, Paywall, or Pricing Table item it belongs to and update the settings there.
