## What is a mapping?

A mapping tells PayGlue: when a customer buys product X from provider Y, give them Ghost membership tier Z.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Mappings">
    In your PayGlue dashboard, go to **Mappings** and click **New mapping**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a provider and product">
    Choose your payment provider and select the product or price you want to map.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Ghost tier">
    Choose which Ghost membership tier the customer should receive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure email">
    Select which welcome email Ghost should send. Magic link is recommended.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and copy the webhook URL">
    Save the mapping. Copy the webhook URL and add it to your provider's webhook settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
