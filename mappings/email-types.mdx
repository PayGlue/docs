## Available types

| Type | Ghost email sent |
| --- | --- |
| `signin` (Magic link) | A login link. Recommended for all tiers. |
| `subscribe` | Free welcome email. |
| `paid-subscribe` | Paid welcome email. |

See [Welcome emails](/ghost/welcome-emails) for full configuration details.
