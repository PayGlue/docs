## Roles

| Role | Access |
| --- | --- |
| Owner | Full access, including billing and organization deletion |
| Member | Access to mappings, credentials, and event log |

## Inviting a team member

Go to **Settings \> Team** and enter the email address of the person you want to invite.

## Removing a team member

Owners can remove members at any time from **Settings \> Team**.
