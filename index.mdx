Welcome to PayGlue. This guide walks you through connecting your Ghost publication to a payment provider so members receive access automatically after a purchase.

## What you need

- A Ghost publication (self-hosted or Ghost Pro)
- A PayGlue account
- An account with a supported payment provider (Polar, Lemon Squeezy, or Stripe)

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Ghost">
    Add your Ghost Admin API key so PayGlue can create and manage members.

    [Connect Ghost](/ghost/connect)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a payment provider">
    Add your provider credentials and configure the webhook URL.

    [View providers](/providers/polar)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a mapping">
    Define which product triggers which Ghost membership tier.

    [Create a mapping](/mappings/create)
  </Step>
</Steps>
