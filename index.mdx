Welcome to PayGlue. This guide walks you through connecting your Ghost publication to a payment provider so members receive access automatically after a purchase.

## What you need

- A Ghost publication (self-hosted or Ghost Pro)
- A PayGlue account
- An account with a supported payment provider (Polar, Lemon Squeezy, or Stripe)\
  \

## **What is PayGlue?**

PayGlue connects Ghost memberships to payment providers like Polar, Lemon Squeezy, and Stripe. When a customer completes a purchase, PayGlue automatically creates or updates their Ghost membership.

## **Do I need a Stripe account?**

Ghost requires Stripe to be connected for its built-in membership features. PayGlue works alongside this — your customers pay through your chosen provider, and PayGlue handles the Ghost membership side. See [Why Ghost requires Stripe](/ghost/stripe-requirement).

## **Which payment providers are supported?**

Currently: Polar. Lemon Squeezy, PayPal are live; Paddle, Mollie, digistore24, copecart, and Gumroad are planned.

**Is my data safe?**

Yes. All credentials are encrypted at rest and PayGlue operates on EU infrastructure. See [Security overview](/security/overview).
