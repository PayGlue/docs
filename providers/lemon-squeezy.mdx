## What to expect

Lemon Squeezy integration will work similarly to Polar:

1. Connect your Lemon Squeezy API key in PayGlue
2. Create a mapping from a Lemon Squeezy product to a Ghost tier
3. Add the PayGlue webhook URL to Lemon Squeezy
