## Email types

When PayGlue creates a Ghost member, it can trigger Ghost to send a welcome email. You choose the email type per mapping.

| Type | When to use |
| --- | --- |
| Magic link (`signin`) | Recommended. Sends a login link. Works for all members. |
| Free welcome (`subscribe`) | For free-tier members. |
| Paid welcome (`paid-subscribe`) | For paid-tier members. |

## How to configure

In your mapping settings, select the email type under **Welcome email**. The default is **Magic link**.

<Note>
  Ghost sends emails from your publication's configured email address. Make sure your email settings are correct in Ghost Admin.
</Note>
