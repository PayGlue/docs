## The short answer

Ghost's built-in membership system is tightly coupled to Stripe. Even if your customers pay through Polar or another provider, Ghost itself needs a Stripe account connected to unlock its membership features.

## What PayGlue does

PayGlue bypasses Ghost's native payment flow entirely. When a customer pays through your chosen provider, PayGlue creates or updates their Ghost membership directly via the Ghost Admin API — no Stripe charge involved.

## What you need to do

1. Connect any Stripe account to Ghost (it does not need to process real payments)
2. Connect your actual payment provider to PayGlue
3. PayGlue handles the rest
